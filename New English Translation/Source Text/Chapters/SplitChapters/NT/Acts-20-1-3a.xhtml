Acts
Chapter 20:1-3a

Paul travels to Greece

1 After the disturbance had ended, Paul sent for the disciples, and after encouraging them and saying farewell, he left to go to Macedonia. 2 After he had gone through those regions and spoken many words of encouragement to the believers there, he came to Greece, 3a where he stayed for three months.


Chapter 3 is split here as Paul continues his third missionary journey. He likely wrote Romans during this period.
